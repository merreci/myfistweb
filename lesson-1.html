Begin with a gretting

Always open your email with a gretting,such as "Dear Lillian". If your relationship whith the reader is
formal,use their family name (eg. "Dear Mrs. Price"). If the relationship is more casual, you can simply say,
"Hi Kelly". If you don't know the name of the person you are writing to, use: "To whom it
may concern" or "Dear Sir/Madam".
